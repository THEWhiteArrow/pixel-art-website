Privacy policy of Pixel Art Creator add-on

What information is needed?

Pictures.

What is the collected information and How is the information used?

In order for add-on to work you must only provide us with pictures. Pictures are not stored or shared.

To whom does Pixel Art Creator share the information?

Your privacy is important to us and we assure that we follow all regulations and standards when it comes to managing customer information. We do not sell, trade or rent your personal information to third parties.

How does Pixel Art Creator protect my data?

Pixel Art Creator is built as a stand-alone app extension that can be installed directly on your account. We do not make any external request and everything you do does not leave the sheets.
